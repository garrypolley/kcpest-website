Termites can damage framing and structural wood without obvious early warning signs. Our termite service is built to identify risk areas and stop active activity before repairs become costly.

## Termite service approach

- Property inspection for signs of termite activity
- Treatment strategy based on structure and infestation level
- Prevention recommendations for moisture and wood-contact risks

## Common warning signs

- Mud tubes on foundation walls
- Hollow-sounding wood
- Swarmers near windows, doors, or lights

If you suspect termite activity, contact us quickly for evaluation and treatment options.
