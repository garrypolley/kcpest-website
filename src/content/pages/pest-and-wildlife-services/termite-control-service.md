Termites can cause major structural damage before obvious signs are visible. KC Pest Experts provides termite inspections and treatment services to identify active problems and protect homes and businesses in the Kansas City area.

## Instant termite control treatment

When active termite pressure is found, we can apply professional termiticide treatments around the structure to create a protective barrier and stop ongoing activity.

## Termite bait station monitoring

For ongoing monitoring and prevention, bait station systems can be installed and checked routinely to detect and control termites before severe structural damage occurs.

## When to call

- Mud tubes near foundations
- Swarming termites around windows or lights
- Soft or hollow-sounding wood

If you suspect termites, schedule an inspection as soon as possible.
