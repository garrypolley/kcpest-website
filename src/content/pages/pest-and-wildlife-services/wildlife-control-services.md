Wildlife intrusions are more than a nuisance. Animals in attics, crawlspaces, and wall voids can create contamination risks and ongoing property damage.

KC Pest Experts provides wildlife-focused service planning for issues involving bats, raccoons, and other nuisance animals, with a focus on restoring a safe living environment.

## Why this matters for family health

Public health guidance from CDC and EPA highlights that animal waste and contaminated dust can increase exposure risks, including:

- **Leptospirosis** risk pathways associated with rodent urine contamination in water, soil, and surfaces
- **Salmonellosis** exposure from environments contaminated by animal droppings
- **Tularemia** risk pathways linked to infected wildlife contact and vectors in certain scenarios
- **Histoplasmosis** concern when bat or bird droppings are disturbed in enclosed spaces

## Our wildlife service priorities

1. Identify and address active intrusion points
2. Reduce contamination and biohazard exposure risk
3. Apply commercial-grade disinfectant treatment in affected attic zones
4. Support a cleaner, safer home environment after removal and cleanup

Our goal is simple: help families get healthy, comfortable, and confident in their homes again.
